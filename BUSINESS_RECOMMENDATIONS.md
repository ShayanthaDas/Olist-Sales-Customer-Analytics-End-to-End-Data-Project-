Olist Sales & Customer Analytics



1. Strengthen Customer Retention Strategy

Customer retention is one of the strongest drivers of revenue in this dataset. Repeat customers contribute significantly to overall sales.

Actions:

* Introduce loyalty or reward programs
* Implement personalized email marketing campaigns
* Offer discounts for repeat purchases
* Segment customers based on purchasing frequency



2. Optimize Product Portfolio

Revenue is concentrated in a limited number of product categories.

Actions:

* Scale high-performing categories
* Reduce focus on low-performing or unprofitable segments
* Introduce cross-selling strategies between categories

---

## 3. Improve Delivery Performance

Delivery delays have a direct negative impact on customer satisfaction scores.

### Actions:

* Optimize logistics partnerships
* Improve seller fulfillment tracking systems
* Introduce delivery time SLAs
* Monitor delayed orders in real-time dashboards

---

## 4. Regional Growth Expansion

Certain geographic regions show significantly higher demand.

### Actions:

* Increase marketing spend in high-growth regions
* Strengthen seller networks in underperforming areas
* Analyze regional demand patterns for inventory planning



5. Data-Driven Decision System

Currently, decision-making is reactive rather than data-driven.

Actions:

* Implement centralized KPI dashboards
* Automate weekly performance reporting
* Enable real-time business monitoring for leadership teams



Expected Impact of These Recommendations

If implemented, the organization can expect:

* Higher customer retention rates
* Increased revenue per customer
* Improved operational efficiency
* Better regional market penetration
* Stronger customer satisfaction scores
